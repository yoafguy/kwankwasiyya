Sen. Rabiu Musa Kwankwaso (Ph.D, FNSE)

Sen. Rabiu Musa Kwankwaso (Ph.D, FNSE), FNSE, FNIQS (born 21 October, 1956) is a Nigerian politician who was the Governor of Kano state from 1999 to 2003 and 2011 to 2015. - After he lost his re-election in 2003, he was appointed the first Minister of Defence of the Fourth Republic with no prior military background from 2003 to 2007, under the administration of former president Olusegun Obasanjo.

He was later elected to the Senate in 2015, serving one term under the platform of the All Progressives Congress (APC) representing Kano Central Senatorial District. He is currently the national leader of the New Nigeria Peoples Party...
